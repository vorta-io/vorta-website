Simple is hard, and we did the hard part. Creating and describing your products is incredibly easy. With our innovative approach you can create your product with all it's SKUs and prices in minutes!

HISTORY TRACKING

Once your product is created we give it Unique Identifier number (UID) and we track full history of it. Changes of inventories, shipments, deliveries. If product is claimed we will know if it is the same product and who was responsible for it.

  • Full product history
  • UID tracking
  • integrated claim support
PER CUSTOMER CUSTOMIZATION

Control your inventory with very simple and granular approach. You are able to setup custom product name, custom product number, minimal order quantity etc. All that you can do per each customer for each one of your SKUs.

  • Granular price control
  • Granular naming control
  • Minimal orders per customer
