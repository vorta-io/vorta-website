Is Vorta for you?

Vorta is intend for small and medium companies looking for efficient and simple solution for managing their supply chains. If your organization is experiencing problems providing accurate order confirmations, organizing their production per demand or reliably delivering goods to their customers than Vorta might do the trick.

What is Vorta?

Vorta provides solutions, that have been since their inception, completely focused on solving problems efficiently and in the the fewest steps possible. This resulted in a system which takes a lot of manual work (even for software) from your hands. These are areas under our control.

By controlling these key areas we provide are able to provide myriad of interesting features. Most importantly we are able to correct you as you move through supply chain network. Not allowing mistakes such as late orders, miss-labeled shipments, incorrect invoicing even to happen.

Features

Current archaic approaches to ERP softwares have left a lot of room for innovation. We at Vorta took it on our selves to innovate and expand on existing solutions. Our mission goal is to be highly efficient & simple in our solutions. Here are some of our features which showcase exactly that goal.

  • Product designer
  • Procurement engine
  • Shipments

With our disruptive product creation process you are able to create product and all it's SKUs in minutes. At the same time defining pricing structure, material consumption and spare parts associations. Easy as it looks.

  • Automatic SKU creation
  • Full and simple pricing control
  • Material consumption control
  • Spare part support
  • Packaging & pallet support
  • Customer specific presentation

Based on your demand you are one click away from procurement of needed goods. We will calculate needed goods taking into account many factors. At the same time taking your current stock into consideration.

  • Information about all unfilled orders
  • Minimal order quantity, lead time comparisons
  • Inventory status
  • Confirmed customer delivery times
  • Automatically create all suppliers invoices
  • Add guards not allowing miss-planing
  • Register goods on production inventories
  • Provide stickers for finished goods

Full culmination of our different integrations is definitely most present in our shipment process. You plan your shipment by setting goods in your truck, all destinations information and needed documentation is automatically provided.

  • Automatic delivery timesheet
  • Automatic invoices between all parties
  • Automatic dispatch notes
  • Guards for planning shipment times
  • Return shipment support
  • Volume, weight calculations
  • Route planning
