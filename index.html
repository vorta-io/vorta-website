Enter the Vorta

Vorta is a cloud software service aimed towards small to medium B2B businesses looking to get help with organization, quality of service, and digitization of supply chain processes. We offer a very straightforward and easy to understand solution that will remove mundane tasks from your company and help you manage logistical challenges with ease.

Innovation and simplicity are our main strengths. We assess your organization and provide consultancy to bring you simple & innovative solution that can be fully implemented in under two months, to learn more check out our case study.

Three steps to Vorta
We start with the vision

Managing expectations is something we take very seriously at Vorta. We work hard to give you a vision of the end goal from the start.

Before you make any kind of commitments you will have a chance to try out your solution first! See your products move through the supply chain from the moment of order to the moment of delivery to the customer. We offer all of this risk-free.

Onboarding made easy

We here at Vorta have always made our primary goal to keep things simple. Our software is easy to use and maintain. Because of that we offer onboarding with maximum duration of two months.
