How Vorta can help your organization

Vorta is created to make your supply chain process easier, more intuitive and fun. We don’t want you to lose time with complicated processes or endless software implementations. You and your colleagues will experience straight forward solutions to set your companies supply chain network up in weeks. Vorta controls following business aspects.

By controlling these key areas we provide are able to provide myriad of interesting features. Most importantly we are able to correct you as you move through supply chain network. Not allowing mistakes such as late orders, miss-labeled shipments, incorrect invoicing even to happen.

Is Vorta for you?

Vorta is intended for small and medium companies looking for efficient and simple solution for managing their supply chain. If your organization is experiencing problems providing accurate order confirmations, organizing their production per demand or reliably delivering goods to their customers than Vorta might do the trick.

  • Automatic SKU creation
  • Full and simple pricing control
  • Material consumption control

  • Information about all unfilled orders

  • Automatic dispatch notes

  • Return shipment support
  • Volume, weight calculations
  • Route planning
